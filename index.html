Our Goals

Milestones for India 2047

In 2047, India aims to achieve a fully developed status by implementing 100% Digital Governance , Green & Connected Transport , Rural-Urban Resilience and ensuring Inclusive Digital Literacy for all citizens. achieve sustainable development, enhance global leadership, and ensure every Indian benefits from the digital revolution by 2047.

100% Digital Governance

Citizen-first government services, with privacy-by-design and AI-assisted decision support to remove bureaucracy and increase transparency.

Inclusive Digital Literacy

Multilingual education programs & local maker hubs to ensure citizens can participate in the digital economy.

Green & Connected Transport

Electrified and shared fleets with intelligent routing, integrated payments, and zero-emission urban cores.

Rural-Urban Resilience

Balanced development through local digital services, climate-resilient agriculture, and telehealth networks.
